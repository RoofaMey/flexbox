St. Dominic

St. Dominic was also called Domingo Felix de Guzman or Dominic of Osma or Caleruega. He was a Catholic priest and founder of the Dominican Order.
He was born on August 8, 1170 in Caleruega, Castile, Spain died on August 6, 1221 at the age of 50 in Bologna, Emilia-Romagna, Italy.
His fiest day is celebrated on August 8 every year in Catholic Church.
